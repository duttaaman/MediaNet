ABOUT MEDIANET


MediaNet is the world leader in online news and information and seeks to inform, engage and empower the world. Staffed 24 hours, seven days a week by a dedicated team in MediaNet bureaus around the world, MediaNet's digital platforms deliver news from almost 4,000 journalists in every corner of the globe.
